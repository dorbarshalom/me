He was later invited by Canva to speak on a panel at their annual conference about this process. Watch the Canva Extend panel: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Sj5jbSQnayI&t=35s
* **Mobile App:** Built D-ID's product mobile app from scratch, reaching 1 million downloads in under a year.
* Both the Canva app and the mobile app remain leading products in D-ID's portfolio today.

### Storydoc | Product Leadership
Dor joined Storydoc at a time when competitors like Gamma were rising fast, making clear differentiation and a carefully chosen battlefield critical. He led a complete redesign and restructuring of the product aimed at winning the SMB B2B market, while running heavy experimentation comparing template-based presentation creation against AI-driven creation. His R&D team was largely offshore, building on his extensive experience managing offshore teams across countries including Ukraine, Poland, and Latvia, among others.

### Wisestamp (part of vCita) | Product Unification & Growth
When Dor joined Wisestamp, the company ran two separate products: a B2C email signature editor and a distinct B2B email signature product. He recognized that by capturing user information from an email signature, the company could offer users much more — a personal webpage (**WiseIntro**), digital business cards (**WiseCards**), and beyond. Wisestamp was acquired by vCita in 2019, becoming part of the company.

One of Dor's major initiatives was unifying the two previously separate products — B2C and B2B — into one. This was a highly complex technical undertaking, since the two products ran on entirely different infrastructure, requiring extensive migration work with close attention to detail. The unification enabled the company to track PQLs (Product Qualified Leads) among B2C users and seamlessly convert them into B2B customers. Previously, converting a user meant manually removing them from one product and re-adding them to the other — creating significant friction.

At Wisestamp, Dor worked closely with CEO and co-founder **Orly Itzhaki**, a true product master with a wide-ranging view across marketing, SEO, business, and finance.
